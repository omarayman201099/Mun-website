CONFERENCES HISTORY

MUN NEW CAIRO ANNUAL CONFERENCE

The main flagship conference of MUN New Cairo, bringing together delegates from diverse backgrounds to participate in in-depth United Nations simulations. The conference focuses on global political, economic, and humanitarian issues, providing a professional environment for debate, negotiation, and resolution writing.

YOUTH MODEL UNITED NATIONS CONFERENCE

A conference designed specifically for first-time and intermediate delegates introducing participants to MUN procedures, and public speaking in a supportive and educational environment.
